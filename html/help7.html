Lemmings™ - Help

Icons Explained

From left to right these are the icons and their alternative functionkeys:

  • Climber - Climbs vertical surfaces (F3)
  • Floater - A brolly opens to ensure a safe descent (F4)
  • Exploder - Bomb to dispatch single Lemmings™ (one way to get rid of Blockers) (F5)
  • Blocker - stands with arms outstretched to block the passage of fellow Lemmings™ (F6)
  • Builder - builds bridges, each limited to twelve steps. When a Lemming has laid his twelfth brick he turns to look at you for a moment... if you click on him again he will continue building. (F7)
  • Basher - digs horizontally, will only dig when a suitible surface is directly ahead of him. (F8)
  • Miner - uses a pick to dig diagonally down (F9)
  • Digger - burrows vertically (F10)
  • Increase flow of Lemmings™ on to the level (F1)
  • Decrease flow of Lemmings™ on to the level up to the displayed minimum (F2)
  • Pause - gives you time to think (F11)
  • Fast Forward - once the road has been laid out by your scouts you can use this button to quickly advance all the remaining lemmings™ to the exit. Note that the time will run faster too!
  • Nuke all lemmings™ - should you find yourself in a 'no-win' situation (doubleclick to activate) (F12)
